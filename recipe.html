macaroni and cheese

Ingredients Directions
4 cups dried macaroni Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
1/2 stick (4 tablespoons) butter Cook the macaroni until still slightly firm. Drain and set aside.
2 1/2 cups whole milk In a small bowl, beat the egg. In a large pot, melt the butter and sprinkle in the flour. Whisk together over medium-low heat. Cook for a couple of minutes, whisking constantly. Don't let it burn. Pour in the milk, add the mustard and whisk until smooth. Cook until very thick, about 5 minutes. Reduce the heat to low.
2 heaping teaspoons dry mustard (more if desired) Take 1/4 cup of the sauce and slowly pour it into the beaten egg, whisking constantly to avoid cooking the eggs. Whisk together until smooth. Pour the egg into the sauce, whisking constantly. Stir until smooth. Add in the cheese and stir to melt. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt and the pepper. Add any additional spices if desired. Taste the sauce and add more salt and seasoned salt as needed! DO NOT UNDERSALT.
1 pound sharp Cheddar, grated (not pre-grated cheese), plus more for baking Pour in the drained, cooked macaroni and stir to combine. Serve immediately (while it's still very creamy) or pour into a buttered baking dish, top with extra cheese and bake until bubbly and golden on top, 20 to 25 minutes.

Creamy Three-Cheese Spaghetti

Ingredients Directions
12 oz. spaghetti In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup pasta water.
1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, 1 minute, then add heavy cream, broth, and 1/2 cup of reserved pasta water. Add cooked spaghetti and toss until noodles are fully coated and liquid is simmering.
3 cloves garlic, minced Remove from heat and stir in cheese. Toss constantly, adding more pasta water until sauce reaches desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper and garnish with chives before serving.

Instant Pot Chicken & Rice

Ingredients Directions
1 tbsp. olive oil Preheat Instant Pot by setting to “Sauté”. Add olive oil and cook onion until slightly softened, 3 to 4 minutes. Add garlic, oregano, and paprika and cook until fragrant, 1 minute.
1/2 yellow onion, chopped Stir in rice and broth and season with salt and pepper. Add carrots, bell pepper, and chicken, and season generously again with salt and pepper.
2 cloves garlic, minced Close lid, change setting to manual or "Pressure Cook" and set for 8 minutes on high. When 8 minutes is up, let pot decompress naturally for 10 minutes, then release pressure, remove lid, and shred chicken. Top with parsley before serving.
